DElate

Late, but never delay!

An App to help you notify other attendees that you're going to be late.

How does it work?


Register

Register events on your Google Calendar


Check

Pop-up notification before an event begins


Notify

Automatically send late notification to all other attendees

Perfect Integration with Google Calendar

Download DElate and sign in with your Google account, and you're all set!

Push-Button Notification

When an event is about to begin, DElate will ask you whether you will be on time or not. If not, you can choose to notify all other attendees with a single click!

Stop Delaying Others

DElate-enabled late notification prevents other attendees from waiting for nothing!
